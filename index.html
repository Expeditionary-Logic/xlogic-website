Our Philosophy

Technology should serve humanity, not the other way around. We're committed to creating software that helps people get what is good from technology while deliberately avoiding the dark patterns and negative impacts that plague the digital world.

Human-Centered Design

We build tools that respect your time, attention, and autonomy. No dark patterns, no manipulation, no endless scrolling.

Privacy First

Your data belongs to you. We design our products to minimize data collection and maximize user privacy.

Purposeful Technology

Every feature we build has a clear purpose: to help you accomplish your goals and enhance your life, not to keep you hooked.
