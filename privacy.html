Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not a commodity. It's a fundamental right.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Expeditionary Logic, we deeply respect your privacy. This isn't just a legal statement—it's a core principle that guides how we build our products. Our approach is simple: we don't need to collect your information, so we don't collect your information.

What We Don't Do

No Tracking

We don't track your behavior, your location, or your usage patterns. No analytics, no cookies, no fingerprinting.

No Data Collection

We don't collect personal information, browsing data, or any other data about you. What you do with our products stays on your device.

No Third Parties

We don't share data with third parties because we don't have any data to share. No advertisers, no data brokers, no hidden partners.

No Accounts Required

Most of our products work without requiring you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

How Our Products Work

Our products, like Sponge, are designed to work entirely on your device. Your audiobooks, your listening progress, your preferences—everything stays local. We've built our technology to be powerful without being invasive.

This means we can't help you recover data if you lose your device, but it also means your data is truly yours. We believe that's a fair trade-off for genuine privacy.

When We Might Collect Information

If you contact us for support or send us feedback, we'll receive whatever information you choose to share with us (like your email address). We'll use that information solely to respond to you and provide support. We won't add you to mailing lists or use it for any other purpose.

Some of our products may be distributed through third-party platforms (like the Apple App Store). Those platforms may collect their own data according to their privacy policies, but we don't receive any personally identifiable information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We'll update this policy if our practices change. But our core commitment won't change: your privacy matters, and we'll always choose privacy over profit.
