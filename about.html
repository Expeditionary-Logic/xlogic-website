About Expeditionary Logic

Building technology that serves humanity

Who We Are

We're a software company dedicated to creating technology for good. In an industry often driven by engagement metrics and data extraction, we've chosen a different path. We build software that respects your time, protects your privacy, and genuinely helps you accomplish your goals.

Our approach is simple: technology should serve people, not exploit them. Every product we create is designed with intention, free from dark patterns, manipulation tactics, and unnecessary data collection. We believe the best software is the kind that helps you live better, learn more, and stay focused on what matters to you.

Our Mission

We're committed to proving that ethical software can thrive without compromising user trust. From free, ad-free applications to privacy-first design principles, everything we build reflects our core belief: good technology creates positive outcomes for the people who use it.

What to Expect

We read every message we receive. While we're a small team and can't always respond immediately, we make it a priority to reply to all inquiries thoughtfully and thoroughly. Feature requests are carefully reviewed and considered in our development roadmap, and bug reports are addressed as quickly as possible.

Your privacy extends to your communications with us. We don't share, sell, or distribute any information you send us. Messages are read only by our team and are used solely to improve our products and provide you with the support or information you need.
